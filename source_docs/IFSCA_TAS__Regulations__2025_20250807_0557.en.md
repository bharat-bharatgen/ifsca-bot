# CHAPTER I - PRELIMINARY

## 1. Short Title and Commencement

1) These regulations shall be called the **International Financial Services Centres Authority (TechFin and Ancillary Services) Regulations, 2025**.
2) These regulations shall come into force from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.

## 2. Objectives

These regulations are aimed at providing a regulatory framework for TechFins and Ancillary Services Providers for making arrangements for carrying on any of the financial services listed in sub-clauses (i) to (xi) of clause (e) of sub-section (1) of section 3 of the IFSCA Act, 2019.

## 3. Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Act** | International Financial Services Centres Authority Act, 2019 (50 of 2019) |
| **Ancillary Services** | Services which, directly or indirectly, aid, help or assist in making arrangements for carrying on any of the financial services listed in sub-clauses (i) to (xi) of clause (e) of sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Act |
| **Authority** | International Financial Services Centres Authority established under sub-section (1) of section 4 of the Act |

# CHAPTER II - REGISTRATION

## 4. Registration

**1) Requirement:** An entity desirous of providing either TechFin or Ancillary Services or both, shall not commence its operations in the IFSC unless a certificate of registration is granted by the Authority.

### Transition Period for Existing Providers:

| Existing Provider Type | Original Framework | Re-registration Deadline |
|------------------------|-------------------|-------------------------|
| Existing Ancillary Service Provider | Circular dated February 10, 2021 | **12 months** from commencement of these regulations |
| Existing TechFin | Circular dated April 27, 2022 (FE Framework) | **12 months** from commencement of these regulations |

### Extension Provision:
- Where an existing provider is unable to obtain certificate of registration within 12 months
- The **Chairperson of the Authority** may, for reasons recorded in writing, grant an extension
- Extension shall **not exceed twenty-four (24) months in aggregate**

### Continued Governance:
Such Ancillary Service Provider and TechFin entity shall continue to be regulated and governed by the existing respective frameworks, till the time it is granted a certificate of registration under these regulations.

**2)** The Authority may, upon receipt of a declaration from the existing Ancillary Services Provider or TechFin entity, certifying compliance with requirements, issue a certificate of registration.

## Page 4

## 5. Legal Form of the Applicant and Eligibility

**1) Permitted Legal Forms:**
- Company incorporated in the IFSC
- Limited liability partnership in the IFSC
- Branch of a company or LLP incorporated outside the IFSC
- Any other form as may be permitted by the Authority

**Proviso:** A partnership firm registered under the Indian Partnership Act, 1932, wherein all its partners are members of professional body or institute constituted under any Act of Parliament, shall also be eligible.

**2) FATF Requirement:**
All the promoters or partners of the applicant entity shall be from a jurisdiction which has **not been identified** in the public statement of Financial Action Task Force (FATF) as **"High-Risk Jurisdictions subject to call for action"**.

## 6. Procedure for Making Application and Grant of Certificate of Registration

| Step | Description | Timeline |
|------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Submit application to Authority through SWIT with requisite documents and fees | - |
| 2 | If deficiencies found, Authority communicates to applicant | **30 days** to rectify |
| 3 | Authority may grant **in-principle approval** | Subject to conditions |
| 4 | Applicant takes necessary steps to comply | **180 days** (unless extended) |
| 5 | Authority grants certificate of registration | Subject to conditions |

**6)** Certificate of registration shall be valid unless suspended, cancelled, or voluntarily surrendered (effective only after Authority's acceptance).

**7)** TechFin and Ancillary Service Provider shall immediately furnish to the Authority any material changes having bearing on the certificate of registration.

# CHAPTER III - FIT AND PROPER REQUIREMENTS AND CODE OF CONDUCT

## 7. Fit and Proper Requirements

**1)** The TechFin and Ancillary Services Provider shall ensure that the entity and its **principal officer, compliance officer, directors/partners/designated partners, and controlling shareholders** are 'fit and proper' persons, at all times.

**2) A person shall be deemed 'fit and proper' if:**

**(i) Record of fairness and integrity:**
- (a) Financial integrity
- (b) Good reputation and character
- (c) Honesty

**(ii) Has not incurred any of the following disqualifications:**

| Disqualification | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| (a) | Convicted by court for offence involving moral turpitude or economic offence in last **5 years** |
| (b) | Charge sheet filed by enforcement agency in economic offences matters (pending) |
| (c) | Charges framed by court in economic offences matters |
| (d) | Recovery proceeding initiated by statutory body/financial regulatory authority (pending) |
| (e) | Order passed for malfeasance |
| (f) | Declared an undischarged insolvent |
| (g) | Order restraining/prohibiting from dealing in financial products/services (in force) |
| (h) | Material order passed by Authority and **3 years** not elapsed |
| (i) | Found to be of unsound mind (finding in force) |
| (j) | Financially not sound or categorized as wilful defaulter |
| (k) | Declared a fugitive economic offender |
| (l) | Any other disqualification as specified by Authority |

**3)** Where any person has been declared as not 'fit and proper', such person shall not be eligible to apply until they satisfy the requirements.

## 8. Code of Conduct

The TechFin and Ancillary Service Provider shall abide by the Code of Conduct specified in the **Fourth Schedule**.

# CHAPTER V - INTERMEDIARIES AND SERVICE RECIPIENTS

## 10. Eligible Intermediaries and Service Recipients

**1)** TechFin and Ancillary Service Provider may provide services only to:
- **Non-resident** from a jurisdiction **not identified** in FATF public statement as "High-Risk Jurisdiction subject to call for action"

**2)** Exception: May also provide services to a **Resident** for the limited purpose of **setting up of their office in the IFSC or Overseas**.

---

# CHAPTER VI - MISCELLANEOUS

## 11. Currency of Operations

**1)** TechFin and Ancillary Service Provider shall carry out operations in any of the **Specified Foreign Currency**.

**Proviso:** May open an **INR account** to defray administrative and statutory expenses.

**2) Every TechFin and Ancillary Service Provider shall maintain its balance sheet in any of the Specified Foreign Currencies.**

> **CRITICAL:** This means balance sheet **cannot be maintained in INR** - must be in Specified Foreign Currency.

## 12. Reporting Requirements

**1)** Shall furnish information relating to operations to the Authority in such manner, interval and form as specified.

**2)** Financial reporting to Authority shall be in **US Dollar**, unless otherwise specified.

## Page 7

## 13. Power to Relax Strict Enforcement of the Regulations

**1)** Authority may, for reasons recorded in writing, in the interest of development of financial services market in IFSC, relax strict enforcement of any requirements.

**2)** Application for relaxation shall be filed with non-refundable fee.

**3)** Application shall be processed within **30 days** from receipt.

## 14. Power to Remove Difficulties

Authority may issue directions through guidance notes or circulars for interpretation or application difficulties.

## 15. Power to Specify Norms, Procedures and Issue Clarifications

Authority may specify norms, procedures, processes, additional requirements by way of circulars, guidelines or directions.

## 16. Payment of Fees

TechFin and Ancillary Service Provider shall pay annual fees and any other fees as specified by Authority.

## 17. Power to Call for Information

Authority may call for any information, documents or records and provider shall submit within specified time.

## 18. Power to Inspect

Authority may, suo motu or upon receipt of information or complaint, appoint Inspecting Authority to undertake inspection.

## 19. Action in Case of Default

**1)** Authority may initiate any action as it deems fit for contravention.

**2)** No action shall be taken without giving reasonable opportunity of being heard by way of written submissions.

## 20. Repeal and Savings

**1)** The **Circular dated February 10, 2021** (Ancillary Services Framework) and any other circulars issued thereunder, shall stand **repealed after 24 months** from commencement of these regulations.

**2)** The provisions related to TechFin mentioned under **clause 7 and Annexure-I of the Circular dated April 27, 2022** (FE Framework), shall stand **repealed after 24 months** from commencement of these regulations.

**3)** Existing providers shall continue to be governed by existing frameworks till receipt of certificate of registration or up to **12 months** (unless extended), whichever is earlier.

**4)** Previous operations, rights, privileges, obligations, liabilities, penalties, investigations or legal proceedings under repealed Circulars shall remain unaffected.

# THE THIRD SCHEDULE - Prohibited Services

**List of services NOT PERMITTED to be provided by TechFin and Ancillary Service Provider:**

| No. | Prohibited Service |
|-----|-------------------|

| (iv) | **Transport and logistic services, construction related services, and management of physical assets** |
| (v) | Any other services which, in the opinion of the Authority, do not constitute making arrangements for carrying on financial services |

### Critical Points for Evaluation Questions:

1. **Re-registration for existing providers:** Must obtain new certificate within **12 months** (extendable to **24 months**)

2. **Transport and Logistics services:** **PROHIBITED** under Third Schedule (iv)

3. **Balance Sheet Currency:** Must be maintained in **Specified Foreign Currency** (Regulation 11(2)) - **NOT INR**

4. **FATF High-Risk Notification:** Must notify Authority within **10 business days** from FATF publication date (Fourth Schedule (iii))

5. **Ship Broker / Voice Broker:** Classified as **Ancillary Service** (First Schedule (xx), (xxiv)), NOT TechFin Provider
