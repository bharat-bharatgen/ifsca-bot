## Background

In view of the recent regulatory developments in the IFSC ecosystem and based on the request received from the market participants for **expanding the scope of activities** covered under "Trusteeship Services" of the IFSCA (Ancillary Services) Framework, 2021, it has been decided to carry out the following amendment.

## Amendment

**Clause 4.3 of Annexure I** to the IFSCA (Ancillary Services) Framework, 2021 shall be **substituted** as under:

### 4.3. Trusteeship Services (Amended)

**(a) Trusteeship Services** in relation to:
- **Alternative Investment Funds**
- **Investment Trusts**
- **Family Investment Funds (FIF)**
- **Security Trust arrangements**
- Any other related services, including **acting as escrow agent**

> **Explanation:** The Investment Trust refers to structures such as:
> - **Infrastructure Investment Trusts (InvITs)**
> - **Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs)**
> - Any other structure of similar nature set up under a Trust

**(b) Trusteeship Services** may also be provided to various schemes, including **retail schemes** launched by the **Fund Management Entities (FMEs)** registered under the **IFSCA (Fund Management) Regulations, 2025**, subject to compliance with:
- **'Fit and proper' criteria**
- **Code of conduct**
- Other applicable compliances specified under the said regulations

## Other Provisions

All other provisions specified in the framework dated February 10, 2021, as amended from time to time, shall remain **unchanged**.

## Legal Authority

This circular is issued in exercise of powers conferred by **section 12 of the IFSCA Act, 2019** to develop and regulate the financial product, financial services and financial institutions in the IFSCs.

## Effective Date

This amendment shall come into force with **immediate effect**.

### Expanded Trusteeship Services Scope

| Service Category | Coverage |
|------------------|----------|
| **Alternative Investment Funds** | Trusteeship services |
| **Investment Trusts** | InvITs, REITs, and similar structures |
| **Family Investment Funds (FIF)** | Trusteeship services |
| **Security Trust arrangements** | Trusteeship services |
| **Escrow Agent** | Acting as escrow agent |
| **Retail Schemes** | FME retail schemes under IFSCA (Fund Management) Regulations, 2025 |

### Compliance Requirements for Retail Schemes Trusteeship

| Requirement | Applicable Regulation |

| Fit and Proper Criteria | IFSCA (Fund Management) Regulations, 2025 |
| Code of Conduct | IFSCA (Fund Management) Regulations, 2025 |
| Other Applicable Compliances | IFSCA (Fund Management) Regulations, 2025 |
