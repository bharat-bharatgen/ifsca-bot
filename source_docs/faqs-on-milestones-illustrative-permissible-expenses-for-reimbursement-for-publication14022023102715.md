## Preliminary

The IFSCA (FinTech Incentive) Scheme, 2022 was notified on **Feb 04, 2022**, with the principal objective to promote the establishment of a world class FinTech Hub by providing financial support to eligible applicants in the form of specific grant(s) as specified in the Scheme.

Under the aforementioned Scheme, eligible applicants can avail the applicable grant on **reimbursement basis**.

This document is to guide applicants with regard to:
- **Part A:** Milestones to be achieved by the applicant(s)
- **Part B:** Illustrative list of permissible expenses for reimbursement

# Part A: Milestones for Each Grant Category

## Q. What are the respective milestones, for each grant category, to be achieved by an applicant for grant disbursement?

### 1. FinTech Start-up Grant (INR 1.5 Million / ₹15 Lacs)

**Purpose:** For developing an MVP (a product or a service) and related 'go-to-market' initiatives for a start-up with a novel FinTech idea or solution.

| Milestone | Description | Disbursement % |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **M1** | Identifying users, market needs and features to be built in the MVP under the IFSCA Sandbox | **30%** |
| **M2** | Tie-up with at least one partner for the completed MVP | **30%** |
| **M3** | Obtaining Limited Use Authorization (Regulatory Sandbox)/Authorization, as applicable under Framework for FinTech Entities in the IFSCs, for the project applied under the Scheme | **40%** |

---

### 2. Proof of Concept (PoC) Grant (INR 5.0 Million / ₹50 Lacs)

**Purpose:** For conducting a PoC, either in Indian markets or overseas, by an early or mature FE in domestic market or overseas.

| Milestone | Description | Disbursement % |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **M1** | Establishing users, market needs and features to be built in the PoC | **30%** |
| **M2** | Tie-up with at least one partner for the completed PoC | **30%** |
| **M3** | Obtaining Limited Use Authorization (Regulatory Sandbox)/Authorization as applicable under Framework for FinTech Entities in the IFSCs, for the project applied under the Scheme | **40%** |

---

### 3. Sandbox Grant (INR 3.0 Million / ₹30 Lacs)

**Purpose:** Shall be utilized by FEs to experiment with innovative products or services in IFSCA Regulatory or Innovation Sandbox.

| Milestone | Description | Disbursement % |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **M1** | Establishing/Identifying users, market needs and features to be built for the product/solution under the IFSCA Sandbox | **30%** |
| **M2** | Tie-up with at least one partner for testing the product/solution | **30%** |
| **M3** | Obtaining Limited Use Authorization (Regulatory Sandbox)/Authorization as applicable under Framework for FinTech Entities in the IFSCs, for the project applied under the Scheme | **40%** |

---

### 4. Green FinTech Grant (INR 7.5 Million / ₹75 Lacs)

**Purpose:** Shall be utilized towards developing solutions facilitating sustainable finance and sustainability linked finance, including 'Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG)' investments.

| Milestone | Description | Disbursement % |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **M1** | Establishing users, market needs and features to be built for product/solution under the IFSCA Sandbox | **30%** |
| **M2** | Tie-up with at least one partner for testing the product/solution | **30%** |
| **M3** | Obtaining Limited Use Authorization (Regulatory Sandbox)/Authorization as applicable under Framework for FinTech Entities in the IFSCs, for the project applied under the Scheme | **40%** |

---

### 5. Accelerator Grant (INR 1.0 Million / ₹10 Lacs)

**Purpose:** Shall be utilized for supporting an eligible Accelerator applicant for capacity building, build capabilities around mentors, bringing investors, bringing more projects or PoC, tie-ups, etc.

| Milestone | Description | Disbursement % |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **M1** | Two weeks post program initiation with the cohort having at least 10 FinTechs/TechFins relevant to IFSC jurisdiction | **50%** |
| **M2** | Minimum 5 completed applications under Framework for FinTech Entities in the IFSCs from the cohort | **50%** |

---

### 6. Listing Support Grant (INR 1.5 Million / ₹15 Lacs)

**Purpose:** Shall be utilized for supporting Domestic FEs aspiring to go for listing on stock exchanges recognized by the Authority.

| Milestone | Description | Disbursement % |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **M1** | **Submission of Draft Offer Document to IFSCA** | **75%** |
| **M2** | **Listing on IFSCA recognized Exchange(s)** | **25%** |

> **CRITICAL for Evaluation:** First disbursement (M1 = 75%) requires submission of **Draft Offer Document**. Final payment (M2 = 25%) requires **proof of successful listing**.

# Part B: Illustrative Permissible Expenses for Reimbursement

## Q. What are the permissible expenses for availing grant on re-imbursement basis?

An illustrative list of permissible expenses towards reimbursement, expenditure category wise, along with acceptable documents under the IFSCA (FinTech Incentive) Scheme, 2022 is presented below.

> **Note:** Certified copies of invoices/bills of purchase/utilization shall be furnished along with original copy of the same. Original copy will be returned to applicant(s) after verification by the Authority. **Only those expenses which are directly related to the Project applied for under the Scheme shall be considered for reimbursement.**

| S. No. | Broad Expense Head | Includes | Acceptable Documents |
|--------|-------------------|----------|---------------------|
| **1** | **Manpower** | Salaries | 1. Salary slips and bank account statement of the Grantee; 2. Employee ID cards of employees |
| **2** | **Hardware, Software and Information Technology related** | - **Laptop, desktop, Computing equipment, servers** and similar computing equipment<br>- Software Simulators & Software Licenses<br>- Software & Hardware for building prototype/MVP and conducting Proof of Concept (POC)<br>- Cloud service<br>- Seeking relevant certification(s) e.g. Cyber, CERT-In<br>- Broadband or Lease-Line | Invoice/bills of purchase/utilization |
| **3** | **Operating, Administration & Maintenance** | Rent, Utilities bills | Rent receipt, utility bill payment receipt |
| **4** | **Consultancy support** | **Nature: Legal, Technical, Design, Patent, Mentoring** | Consultancy fee payment receipt or bank account statement |
| **5** | **Marketing & Media** | - For all grantee(s): expenses related to booth/stall setup at trade shows, exhibitions, conferences<br>- **For Listing Grant:** expenses related to activities related to preparation of Draft Offer Document to be submitted to IFSCA | Invoices/bills/receipts of the mentioned expenses |

## Key Information Summary

### Milestone Distribution by Grant Type

| Grant Type | Max Amount | M1 | M2 | M3 |
|------------|------------|----|----|----|
| FinTech Start-up Grant | ₹15 Lacs (1.5M) | 30% | 30% | 40% |
| PoC Grant | ₹50 Lacs (5.0M) | 30% | 30% | 40% |
| Sandbox Grant | ₹30 Lacs (3.0M) | 30% | 30% | 40% |
| Green FinTech Grant | ₹75 Lacs (7.5M) | 30% | 30% | 40% |
| Accelerator Grant | ₹10 Lacs (1.0M) | 50% | 50% | - |
| **Listing Support Grant** | ₹15 Lacs (1.5M) | **75%** | **25%** | - |

### Listing Support Grant - Critical Details

| Milestone | Requirement | Disbursement |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| **M1 (First/75%)** | **Submission of Draft Offer Document to IFSCA** | 75% of grant |
| **M2 (Final/25%)** | **Listing on IFSCA recognized Exchange(s)** (Proof of successful listing) | 25% of grant |

### Permissible Expenses Summary

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Manpower** | Salaries |
| **Hardware, Software & IT** | **Laptops, desktops, computing equipment, servers**, software licenses, cloud services, certifications |
| **Operating/Admin** | Rent, utilities |
| **Consultancy** | **Legal, Technical, Design, Patent, Mentoring** fees |
| **Marketing & Media** | Trade shows, exhibitions, Draft Offer Document preparation |

## Critical Points for Evaluation Questions

1. **Listing Support Grant M1 (75%):** Requires submission of **Draft Offer Document** to IFSCA

2. **Listing Support Grant M2 (25%):** Requires **Proof of successful listing on Stock Exchange**

3. **Green FinTech Grant:** Maximum **₹75 Lacs (INR 7.5 Million)** for developing solutions facilitating sustainable finance, sustainability linked finance, including **ESG investments**

4. **Hardware/IT Expenses Reimbursable:** **Laptop and Server costs** are included under permissible expenses (Hardware, Software and Information Technology related)

5. **Consultancy Fees Reimbursable:** **Legal, Technical, Design, Patent, Mentoring** fees can be claimed for reimbursement under all grant categories
